Emerald Ash Bore

What is the Emerald Ash Bore?

The Emerald Ash Bore is an exotic insect pest from asia. the larval stage feeds below the bark of trees on the vessels that conduct water and nutrients through the tree, causing tree death.

Our Pest Services

Jedd's Tree Care offers an insecticide treatment for ash trees affected by the Emerald Ash Bore. In this treatment, multiple rounds of insecticide will be let into the soil around the ash tree. the insecticide will absorb into the tree, and kill the feeding larva.
